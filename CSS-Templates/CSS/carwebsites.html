Life is all about choices. You chose the way of life you want to live. It means sacrificing things for the sake of the things which we want to do in life, whether you want to be watch movies with friends, or hang out with them or sit alone in a room and write your story. The choice is to be made and it is you who has to!

People say for writing you need to write then only you can write something. But I think one has to a good reader first then only that person can be a good writer. These two things are the best things as they neither leave you nor they ever betrays you. The thing which we read impacts our life in a very profound way, so we should always read things which benefits us and which help us grow in our life. It is like a drug if you can make yourself better with it or you can make yourself in worse condition. What goes in our mind becomes our thoughts and soon our thoughts becomes our actions. So, we need to be careful about what we are reading!
